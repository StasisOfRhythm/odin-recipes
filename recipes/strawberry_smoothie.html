Strawberry Smoothie

Description

Strawberries blended with milk, yogurt, sugar and vanilla. A tasty treat!

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into glass and serve immediately
